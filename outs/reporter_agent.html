Introduction

N AHMED N requires training in various topics, including data analysis, programming, and project management. The scope of the course search includes online platforms such as Udemy, Coursera, and edX. The intended audience for this report is the procurement team, and the application of the report is to inform the procurement decision-making process.

Methodology

The search criteria used to evaluate the courses included the topic, platform, price, and ratings. The evaluation metrics used to assess the course quality included the number of reviews, the average rating, and the update frequency. The price normalization approach used was to calculate the cost per hour of training.

The data collection timeframe was 2 weeks, and the limitations of the study include the limited number of courses evaluated and the reliance on publicly available data.
